About our Hotel

Step off your flight into the resort ambience of Crowne Plaza Changi Airport, located at Terminal 3 and seamlessly connected to all airport terminals and Jewel, a tropical wonderland where nature meets retail, next to the hotel. Simply take a sky train or link bridge from your terminal directly to our doorstep.

The hotel has won the World's Best Airport Hotel in the 2023 Skytrax World Airport Awards for 8 consecutive years as well as numerous local and international awards.
